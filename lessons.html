Lessons

Student Lessons

Jessica offers lessons ranging from full hour to 15 minutes on a weekly basis. Typically for younger students, less is more. Most of her students take 30-45 minute lessons. Students don't need any previous experience to start lessons, just a piano and a willingness to learn!

Traveling

Jessica will travel to students homes for lessons given that they have a full size weighted keyboard or piano. She also offers lessons out of her Wicker Park home, where she has a 5 foot Yamaha grand piano, at a significantly discounted price.

Lesson Fees

Lessons fees differ depending on travel time and the number of students at a location or within a family. Jessica's base rate is based on her experience. Once your begin lessons your rate never changes for as long as you are a student.
